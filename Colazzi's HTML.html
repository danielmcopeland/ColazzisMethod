Colaizzi's Method

Below is a comparison of two methods of data analysis taken from an opinion survey on salary ranges for music therapists. Responses came from health administrators and health administration students. Colaizzi's Method identifies themes in responses and compares them based on their frequency. Since this method sometimes pulls multiple pieces of data from individual responses, some responses become weighted more heavily than others. This is effective for qualitative data and finding themes in written responses, but can cause inaccuracies when dealing with relational data (especially numerical data).
